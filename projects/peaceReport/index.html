Peace Report

Technologies used:

PeaceReport is an open-source codebase for independent journalists to share unbiased news and stories about conflicts, promoting understanding and peace.
The project includes basic features like signing up, logging in, creating, editing, reading, and deleting articles.
Licensed under MIT, PeaceReport is free and open for anyone to build upon, making it accessible for developers to enhance and expand its functionality.
